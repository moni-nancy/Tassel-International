Our Mission

Since our inception in 2023, Tassel International has been unwavering in its commitment to transforming the way couples preserve and share their love stories. We believe that every love story deserves to be cherished and celebrated, and our platform empowers couples to do just that. At the heart of our mission lies the desire to alleviate the burdens often associated with preserving and sharing precious memories.

With Tassel International, couples can effortlessly capture, edit, and share their love stories with the world. Our user-friendly platform eliminates the complexities of video editing, collaborating and sharing, allowing couples to focus on what truly matters: preserving their love story together.
We believe that every couple's love story is unique and deserves to be told in a way that is both meaningful and enduring.
